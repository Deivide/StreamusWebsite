    Getting Started*

    *Not complete yet, pardon the ugly, but wanted to share the info asap

    Just installed? Welcome to your new content library.

    Streamus starts pretty much empty. You've gotta add some videos to your playlist! Search just like you would on YouTube or copy & paste a YouTube video URL.

    Streaming videos. Sounds like what you want, right?

    Click on a video to add it to the stream. For playlists, right-click the one you want and select 'Add Playlist to Stream.' Either way, just click play to start streaming YouTube!

    Discover! Imagine if Pandora and YouTube had a baby.

    Click the radio tower to enable radio mode. Streamus will suggest new YouTube videos whenever you play past the end of the stream!

    Keybindings! Because opening Streamus is too much work.

    Streamus supports a bunch of keyboard shortcuts. You can open the interface, skip between videos, etc. Set 'em up to make your life even easier.

    Omnibox Stream videos right meow!

    You can use the omnibox to immediately stream videos in Streamus. Just type 'streamus', tap your spacebar and you're off searching YouTube. Simple, huh?
